Our Story

How ten strangers
became a family.

{% endblock %} {% block content %}

It all began in the autumn of 2019, when ten freshmen walked into the same lecture hall — Introduction to Something None of Us Remember. We bonded over bad cafeteria food, shared notes, and the universal fear of not making friends.

What started as a study group became a dinner group. The dinner group became a road trip group. And somewhere between all-nighters, inside jokes, and accidental matching pyjamas, we became a family.

Four years, countless memories, and one global pandemic later, we graduated. But unlike most university groups that drift apart, we stayed close. This website is our way of keeping the story going.

This is only the beginning.
