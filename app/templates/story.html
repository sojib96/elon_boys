Our Story

How ten strangers
became a family.

A story that began in a lecture hall and continues to this day.

Autumn 2019 — The First Day

It all began in the autumn of 2019, when ten freshmen walked into the same lecture hall — Introduction to Something None of Us Remember. We bonded over bad cafeteria food, shared notes, and the universal fear of not making friends. None of us knew we’d just met our best friends.

2
02

2020 — The Bond Deepens

What started as a study group became a dinner group. The dinner group became a road trip group. We spent late nights in the library, celebrated birthdays together, and somewhere between inside jokes and accidental matching pyjamas, we became a family. The pandemic only brought us closer — our group chat became a lifeline.

3
03

2022 — The House Share

We moved into our first shared house. The boiler broke on day one. We called it home anyway. That house saw everything — all-nighters before exams, impromptu dance parties in the kitchen, a legendary birthday surprise, and more pizza deliveries than any of us care to admit. It was chaotic, messy, and absolutely perfect.

4
04

2023 — Graduation & Beyond

Four years, countless memories, and one global pandemic later, we graduated. Caps in the air, tears in our eyes, and one last group photo that still makes us smile. But unlike most university groups that drift apart, we stayed close. This website is our way of keeping the story going.

🌟

This is only the beginning. The story continues — and you’re invited to be part of it.
